Hackathons

UA Innovate 2024: Fintech

Our team created an application to help users track their spending and make informed financial decisions. We used the OpenAI API to provide users with recommendations on how they can cut spending, save up for a big purchase, or make other important financial decisions. We additionally provided users with visualizations of their spending, so they could further see possible areas where they could cut expenses.
